## Getting Started

### Creating Your Supplier Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to the marketplace homepage
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Register"
   - Select "Supplier Account"

2. **Basic Information**
   - Email address (your login username)
   - Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and last name
   - Phone number
   - Country and location

3. **Business Information**
   - Business name and type
   - Store name (this will be your public store name)
   - Business registration details
   - Tax identification numbers

4. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click to verify your account
   - Your account will be pending approval

### Account Approval Process

1. **Document Submission**
   - Business license
   - Tax registration certificate
   - Identity verification documents
   - Bank account information

2. **Verification Timeline**
   - Initial review: 1-3 business days
   - Additional documentation requests: 1-2 days
   - Final approval: 1-2 business days
   - Total process: 3-7 business days

3. **Approval Status**
   - **Pending**: Under review
   - **Approved**: Ready to start selling
   - **Rejected**: Requires additional documentation
   - **Suspended**: Account temporarily disabled

## Account Setup and Verification

1. **Company Information**

   - **Store Description**: Brief description of your business

2. **Contact Details**
   - **Contact Person**: Primary contact name and position
   - **Business Address**: Complete business address
   - **Phone Numbers**: Primary and alternative numbers

3. **Business Capabilities**

2. **Business Verification**
   - Business license verification

## RFQ Management

### Understanding RFQs

1. **What are RFQs?**
   - Request for Quotations from buyers
   - Detailed specifications and requirements
   - Quantity and delivery requirements
   - Budget and timeline information

2. **RFQ Matching**
   - System matches RFQs to your products
   - Based on categories and capabilities
   - Filtered by your business profile
   - Prioritized by relevance score

### Responding to RFQs

1. **RFQ Evaluation**
   - Review buyer requirements carefully
   - Check if you can meet specifications
   - Verify quantity and timeline feasibility
   - Assess profitability and fit

2. **Creating Quotations**
   - **Pricing**: Competitive and profitable pricing
   - **MOQ**: Minimum order quantity requirements
   - **Lead Time**: Realistic production and delivery time
   - **Payment Terms**: Your preferred payment terms
   - **Validity Period**: How long the quote is valid
   - **Terms & Conditions**: Additional terms and conditions

3. **Quotation Best Practices**
   - Respond quickly (within 24 hours)
   - Be specific and detailed
   - Include all costs (shipping, taxes, etc.)
   - Attach relevant documents or certificates
   - Follow up professionally

### RFQ Analytics

1. **Performance Metrics**
   - **RFQ Views**: Number of RFQs you've viewed

   - **Win Rate**: Percentage of quotations that become orders
   - **Average Response Time**: How quickly you respond
   - **Competition Analysis**: How your quotes compare

2. **Improvement Strategies**
   - Analyze lost opportunities
   - Improve response times
   - Optimize pricing strategies
   - Enhance quotation quality

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Account and Verification Issues

**Issue**: Account approval taking too long
**Solutions**:
- Check document quality and completeness
- Respond promptly to verification requests
- Contact support for status updates
- Ensure all required documents are submitted

**Issue**: Verification documents rejected
**Solutions**:
- Review rejection reasons carefully
- Resubmit with higher quality documents
- Provide additional documentation if requested
- Contact support for clarification

#### Product Management Issues

**Issue**: Products not appearing in search results
**Solutions**:
- Check product approval status
- Optimize product titles and descriptions
- Ensure products are published and active
- Review category assignments

**Issue**: Low product visibility
**Solutions**:
- Improve product SEO optimization
- Add more detailed specifications
- Use high-quality images
- Consider featured listing options

#### Order and Communication Issues

**Issue**: Not receiving enough inquiries
**Solutions**:
- Improve product listings quality
- Optimize pricing strategy
- Enhance store profile
- Increase response rate and speed

**Issue**: High inquiry-to-order conversion problems
**Solutions**:
- Improve quotation quality
- Respond faster to inquiries
- Offer competitive pricing
- Provide better customer service

#### Technical Issues

**Issue**: Platform functionality problems
**Solutions**:
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser or device
- Check internet connection
- Contact technical support

**Issue**: File upload problems
**Solutions**:
- Check file size and format requirements
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try uploading one file at a time
- Contact support if issues persist
