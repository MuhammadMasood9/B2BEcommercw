## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to the marketplace homepage
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Register"
   - Select "Buyer Account"

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - Email address (will be your login username)
   - Secure password (minimum 8 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols)
   - First and last name
   - Company name
   - Phone number

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email for a verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account
   - You can now log in to the platform

### First Login

1. **Access the Login Page**
   - Click "Login" on the homepage
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"

2. **Complete Your Profile**
   - You'll be prompted to complete your buyer profile
   - Add company details, industry, and business type
   - This information helps suppliers understand your needs

## Product Discovery

### Browsing Products

1. **Using the Product Catalog**
   - Navigate to "Products" from the main menu
   - Browse by categories or use the search function
   - View product listings with key information

2. **Advanced Search Filters**
   - **Category**: Filter by product categories
   - **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum prices
   - **MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)**: Filter by order quantity requirements
   - **Supplier Location**: Filter by supplier country or region
   - **Verification Status**: Show only verified suppliers
   - **Certifications**: Filter by required certifications (CE, ISO, etc.)
   - **Trade Assurance**: Show only products with trade protection
   - **Sample Availability**: Filter products with samples available

3. **Sorting Options**
   - **Relevance**: Best match for your search terms
   - **Price**: Low to high or high to low
   - **MOQ**: Minimum order quantity
   - **Newest**: Recently added products
   - **Popularity**: Most viewed products
   - **Supplier Rating**: Highest rated suppliers first

### Product Details

1. **Product Information**
   - Detailed product descriptions
   - Technical specifications
   - Multiple product images and videos
   - Available colors, sizes, and variants

2. **Supplier Information**
   - Supplier business name and store
   - Verification level and badges
   - Supplier rating and reviews
   - Response rate and time
   - Location and contact information

3. **Pricing and Terms**
   - Quantity-based pricing tiers
   - Minimum order quantities (MOQ)
   - Lead times and delivery information
   - Payment terms accepted
   - Sample availability and pricing

### Saving and Organizing

1. **Favorites**
   - Click the heart icon to save products
   - Access saved products from "My Favorites"
   - Organize favorites into custom lists

2. **Comparison Tool**
   - Select multiple products to compare
   - View side-by-side specifications
   - Compare pricing and supplier information

## Request for Quotations (RFQs)

### Creating an RFQ

1. **Start a New RFQ**
   - Click "Create RFQ" from the main menu
   - Or click "Request Quote" on a product page

2. **RFQ Details**
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive title for your request
   - **Category**: Select the appropriate product category
   - **Description**: Detailed description of your requirements
   - **Specifications**: Technical requirements and preferences
   - **Quantity**: Required quantity
   - **Target Price**: Your expected price per unit (optional)
   - **Budget Range**: Your total budget range (optional)

3. **Delivery Information**
   - **Delivery Location**: Where products should be delivered
   - **Required Delivery Date**: When you need the products
   - **Payment Terms**: Your preferred payment terms

4. **Additional Settings**
   - **Expiration Date**: When the RFQ expires (default 30 days)
   - **Visibility**: Public or private RFQ
   - **Attachments**: Upload specification documents or images

### Managing Your RFQs

1. **RFQ Dashboard**
   - Access "My RFQs" from the main menu
   - View all your active and closed RFQs
   - Filter by status, category, or date

2. **RFQ Status Types**
   - **Open**: Accepting quotations from suppliers
   - **Closed**: No longer accepting quotations
   - **Expired**: Automatically closed after expiration date

3. **RFQ Actions**
   - **View Details**: See full RFQ information and received quotations
   - **Edit**: Modify RFQ details (only for open RFQs)
   - **Close**: Stop accepting new quotations
   - **Extend**: Extend the expiration date

### Receiving Quotations

1. **Quotation Notifications**
   - Email notifications for new quotations
   - In-app notifications with quotation details
   - Dashboard alerts for urgent responses

2. **Reviewing Quotations**
   - Compare pricing and terms
   - Review supplier credentials
   - Check delivery timelines
   - Evaluate payment terms

## Order Management

### Placing Orders

1. **Order from Quotation**
   - Accept a quotation to create an order
   - Review order details and terms
   - Confirm shipping and billing addresses
   - Select payment method

2. **Direct Product Orders**
   - Add products to cart from product pages
   - Specify quantities and variants
   - Proceed through checkout process

3. **Multivendor Orders**
   - Orders from multiple suppliers are split automatically
   - Each supplier receives their portion
   - Track each order separately

### Order Tracking

1. **Order Status Updates**
   - **Pending**: Order placed, awaiting supplier confirmation
   - **Confirmed**: Supplier has confirmed the order
   - **Processing**: Order is being prepared
   - **Shipped**: Order has been dispatched
   - **Delivered**: Order received at destination
   - **Cancelled**: Order cancelled by buyer or supplier

2. **Order Dashboard**
   - View all your orders in one place
   - Filter by status, supplier, or date
   - Track delivery progress
   - Download order documents

3. **Communication with Suppliers**
   - Message suppliers about order status

### Payment and Invoicing

2. **Invoice Management**

## Dispute Resolution

### When to Create a Dispute

1. **Valid Dispute Reasons**
   - Product quality issues
   - Wrong items received
   - Shipping delays beyond agreed terms
   - Payment or billing issues
   - Communication problems

2. **Before Creating a Dispute**
   - Try to resolve with supplier directly
   - Document all communication
   - Gather evidence (photos, documents)
   - Review order terms and conditions

### Creating a Dispute

1. **Dispute Process**
   - Go to your order details
   - Click "Create Dispute"
   - Select dispute type and reason
   - Provide detailed description
   - Upload supporting evidence

2. **Required Information**
   - Order number and details
   - Specific issue description
   - Evidence (photos, documents, emails)
   - Desired resolution

### Dispute Resolution Process

1. **Dispute Stages**
   - **Open**: Dispute created, supplier can respond
   - **Under Review**: Admin is investigating
   - **Mediation**: Admin is mediating between parties
   - **Resolved**: Solution agreed upon
   - **Closed**: Dispute completed

2. **Admin Mediation**
   - Platform admins review evidence
   - Facilitate communication between parties
   - Propose fair solutions
   - Process refunds if necessary

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems

**Issue**: Cannot log in to account
**Solutions**:
- Check email and password spelling
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if account is locked

**Issue**: Email verification not received
**Solutions**:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Request new verification email
- Ensure email address is correct
- Contact support for manual verification

#### Product Search Issues

**Issue**: Cannot find specific products
**Solutions**:
- Try different search terms
- Use broader categories
- Check spelling and filters
- Browse by category instead
- Contact suppliers directly

**Issue**: Filters not working properly
**Solutions**:
- Clear all filters and start over
- Refresh the page
- Try different filter combinations
- Use basic search instead

#### RFQ and Quotation Issues

**Issue**: Not receiving quotations for RFQs
**Solutions**:
- Review RFQ details for clarity
- Extend expiration date
- Broaden specifications
- Check if requirements are realistic
- Contact suppliers directly

**Issue**: Quotations seem too high
**Solutions**:
- Compare with market prices
- Negotiate with suppliers
- Adjust quantity requirements
- Consider different specifications
- Request multiple quotations

#### Order and Payment Issues

**Issue**: Order status not updating
**Solutions**:
- Contact supplier for updates
- Check communication messages
- Allow time for processing
- Contact admin support if delayed

**Issue**: Payment problems
**Solutions**:
- Verify payment method details
- Check bank/card limits
- Contact payment provider
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact admin support

#### Communication Issues

**Issue**: Suppliers not responding
**Solutions**:
- Check time zones for response delays
- Send follow-up messages
- Try different communication methods
- Be patient with response times
- Contact admin if no response after 48 hours

**Issue**: Chat messages not sending
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Contact technical support
